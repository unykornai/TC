## ARTICLE 1: DEFINITIONS AND INTERPRETATION

### 1.1 Definitions

"**Advance**" means any borrowing under the Facility.

"**Borrowing Base**" means, as of any date of determination, the sum of the Collateral Values of all Eligible Collateral, calculated in accordance with the Borrowing Base Policy.

"**Borrowing Base Certificate**" means a certificate in substantially the form of Exhibit A.

"**Business Day**" means any day other than Saturday, Sunday, or a day on which banks are closed in New York, New York.

"**Collateral**" means all property pledged to secure the Obligations, as more particularly described in the Security Agreement.

"**Collateral Value**" means, with respect to any item of Eligible Collateral, the Face Value thereof multiplied by (1 minus the applicable Haircut).

"**Coverage Ratio**" means, as of any date, the ratio of (a) the Borrowing Base to (b) the sum of all outstanding Advances and LC Exposure.

"**Default**" means any event which, with notice or passage of time or both, would constitute an Event of Default.

"**Eligible Collateral**" means Collateral meeting all eligibility requirements set forth in the Borrowing Base Policy.

"**Event of Default**" has the meaning set forth in Section 7.1.

"**Face Value**" means the stated principal amount of a bond or note.

"**Facility Amount**" means $[FACILITY_AMOUNT].

"**Haircut**" means the percentage discount applied to Face Value as set forth in the Borrowing Base Policy.

"**LC Exposure**" means, at any time, the sum of (a) the aggregate undrawn amount of all outstanding Letters of Credit and (b) the aggregate amount of all unreimbursed LC Disbursements.

"**Letter of Credit**" or "**LC**" means any standby letter of credit issued under this Agreement.

"**Loan Documents**" means this Agreement, the Security Agreement, the Control Agreement, and all other documents executed in connection herewith.

"**Maturity Date**" means [MATURITY_DATE].

"**Obligations**" means all obligations of the Borrower to the Lender under the Loan Documents.

"**Security Agreement**" means the Security Agreement of even date herewith between the Borrower and the Lender.

### 1.2 Interpretation

(a) Words importing the singular include the plural and vice versa.

(b) References to "dollars" or "$" are to United States dollars.

(c) Headings are for convenience only and do not affect interpretation.

## ARTICLE 2: THE FACILITY

### 2.1 Commitment

Subject to the terms and conditions hereof, the Lender agrees to make Advances to the Borrower from time to time during the Availability Period in an aggregate principal amount not to exceed the lesser of:

(a) the Facility Amount; and

(b) the Borrowing Base.

### 2.2 Revolving Nature

Within the limits of this Article 2, the Borrower may borrow, repay, and reborrow Advances.

### 2.3 Letters of Credit

(a) Subject to the terms hereof, the Lender agrees to issue Letters of Credit for the account of the Borrower from time to time during the Availability Period.

(b) The aggregate LC Exposure shall not exceed $[LC_SUBLIMIT].

(c) No Letter of Credit shall have an expiry date later than [LC_MATURITY_LIMIT].

### 2.4 Availability Period

The Facility shall be available from the Closing Date until the earlier of:

(a) the Maturity Date; and

(b) the termination of the Facility pursuant to Article 7.

## ARTICLE 3: BORROWING PROCEDURES

### 3.1 Advance Requests

(a) The Borrower may request an Advance by delivering to the Lender a borrowing notice substantially in the form of Exhibit B ("Borrowing Notice") not later than [NOTICE_PERIOD] Business Days prior to the proposed Advance date.

(b) Each Borrowing Notice shall specify:
   - (i) the requested Advance date;
   - (ii) the principal amount of the requested Advance;
   - (iii) the applicable Interest Period; and
   - (iv) wire instructions for funding.

### 3.2 Minimum Amounts

Each Advance shall be in a minimum amount of $[MIN_ADVANCE] and integral multiples of $[ADVANCE_INCREMENT] in excess thereof.

### 3.3 Funding

Subject to satisfaction of the conditions precedent, the Lender shall make each Advance available to the Borrower by wire transfer to the account specified in the Borrowing Notice.

## ARTICLE 4: INTEREST AND FEES

### 4.1 Interest Rate

(a) Each Advance shall bear interest at a rate per annum equal to [RATE_BENCHMARK] plus [MARGIN]% (the "Interest Rate").

(b) Interest shall be calculated on the basis of a 360-day year and actual days elapsed.

### 4.2 Default Interest

Upon the occurrence and during the continuance of an Event of Default, all Obligations shall bear interest at a rate equal to the Interest Rate plus [DEFAULT_MARGIN]% per annum.

### 4.3 Interest Payments

Accrued interest on each Advance shall be payable:

(a) on the last day of each Interest Period;

(b) upon prepayment; and

(c) on the Maturity Date.

### 4.4 Commitment Fee

The Borrower shall pay to the Lender a commitment fee equal to [COMMITMENT_FEE_RATE]% per annum on the average daily unused portion of the Facility, payable quarterly in arrears.

### 4.5 LC Fees

(a) The Borrower shall pay to the Lender an LC fee equal to [LC_FEE_RATE]% per annum on the daily amount available to be drawn under each Letter of Credit.

(b) The Borrower shall pay standard issuance and amendment fees.

---

## ARTICLE 5: REPAYMENT AND PREPAYMENT

### 5.1 Maturity

All outstanding Advances, together with accrued interest and other Obligations, shall be due and payable on the Maturity Date.

### 5.2 Mandatory Prepayment

(a) If at any time the sum of outstanding Advances and LC Exposure exceeds the Borrowing Base, the Borrower shall immediately prepay Advances and/or cash collateralize LC Exposure in an amount sufficient to eliminate such excess.

(b) If the Coverage Ratio falls below [MIN_COVERAGE_RATIO]%, the Borrower shall prepay or provide additional Collateral within [CURE_PERIOD] Business Days.

### 5.3 Voluntary Prepayment

The Borrower may prepay any Advance in whole or in part at any time without premium or penalty, upon [PREPAYMENT_NOTICE] Business Days' prior notice.

### 5.4 Application of Payments

All payments shall be applied first to fees and expenses, then to accrued interest, then to principal.

## ARTICLE 7: EVENTS OF DEFAULT

### 7.1 Events of Default

Each of the following shall constitute an Event of Default:

(a) **Payment Default**: Failure to pay any principal when due, or any interest or fees within [GRACE_PERIOD] days of when due.

(b) **Representation Default**: Any representation or warranty proves materially false when made.

(c) **Covenant Default**: Breach of any covenant, not cured within [COVENANT_CURE] days after notice.

(d) **Cross Default**: Default under any other indebtedness exceeding $[CROSS_DEFAULT_THRESHOLD].

(e) **Judgment**: Entry of a judgment exceeding $[JUDGMENT_THRESHOLD] not discharged within [JUDGMENT_PERIOD] days.

(f) **Bankruptcy**: Borrower becomes subject to any bankruptcy or insolvency proceeding.

(g) **Collateral Impairment**: Any event materially impairing the value of the Collateral or the Lender's security interest.

(h) **Control Agreement Breach**: Breach of the Control Agreement by Borrower or transfer agent.

(i) **Coverage Ratio**: Coverage Ratio falls below [MIN_COVERAGE_RATIO]% and is not cured within [CURE_PERIOD] Business Days.

### 7.2 Remedies

Upon an Event of Default, the Lender may:

(a) declare all Obligations immediately due and payable;

(b) terminate the Facility;

(c) exercise all rights under the Security Agreement and Control Agreement;

(d) direct STC to transfer the Collateral; and

(e) exercise any other remedies available at law or equity.

## ARTICLE 9: COVENANTS

### 9.1 Affirmative Covenants

The Borrower shall:

(a) Maintain the Collateral free of liens other than the Lender's security interest.

(b) Deliver monthly Borrowing Base Certificates by the 5th Business Day of each month.

(c) Deliver quarterly financial statements within 45 days of each quarter end.

(d) Maintain insurance on the Collateral satisfactory to the Lender.

(d) Incur additional indebtedness secured by the Collateral.

(e) Amend organizational documents in any manner adverse to the Lender.

### 9.3 Financial Covenants
