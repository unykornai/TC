# SECURITY AGREEMENT
## Pledge and Security Agreement

---

**THIS SECURITY AGREEMENT** (this "Agreement") is made as of February 3, 2026

**BETWEEN:**

**(1) OPTKAS1-MAIN SPV**, a Wyoming series limited liability company (the "Debtor" or "Pledgor")

**AND**

**(2) SECURE CAPITAL PARTNERS, LLC**, a Delaware limited liability company (the "Secured Party")

---

## RECITALS

**A.** The Debtor and the Secured Party are parties to a Facility Agreement of even date herewith (as amended, restated, or modified from time to time, the "Facility Agreement").

**B.** As a condition to extending credit under the Facility Agreement, the Secured Party has required the Debtor to execute and deliver this Agreement to secure the Debtor's obligations under the Facility Agreement.

**C.** The Debtor owns certain TC Advantage 5% Secured Medium Term Notes, CUSIP 87225HAB4, which it desires to pledge as collateral.

---

## ARTICLE 1: DEFINITIONS

### 1.1 Defined Terms

Capitalized terms used but not defined herein shall have the meanings given to them in the Facility Agreement.

"**Collateral**" has the meaning set forth in Section 2.1.

"**Control Agreement**" means the Account Control Agreement among the Debtor, the Secured Party, and Securities Transfer Corporation.

"**Obligations**" means all present and future obligations of the Debtor to the Secured Party under the Loan Documents, including principal, interest, fees, expenses, indemnities, and any other amounts owing.

"**Pledged Securities**" means the securities described in Schedule I hereto, together with any additional securities pledged hereunder.

"**Proceeds**" has the meaning given to such term in the UCC.

"**Transfer Agent**" means Securities Transfer Corporation, or any successor transfer agent.

"**UCC**" means the Uniform Commercial Code as in effect in the State of Wyoming.

---

## ARTICLE 2: GRANT OF SECURITY INTEREST

### 2.1 Pledge and Grant

As security for the prompt and complete payment and performance of all Obligations, the Debtor hereby pledges, assigns, and grants to the Secured Party a continuing first priority security interest in all of the Debtor's right, title, and interest in, to, and under the following property, whether now owned or hereafter acquired (collectively, the "Collateral"):

(a) **Pledged Securities**: All securities, instruments, and investment property described in Schedule I, including without limitation:

   - TC Advantage 5% Secured Medium Term Notes
   - CUSIP: 87225HAB4
   - ISIN: US87225HAB42
   - Face Value: $10,000,000
   - Maturity: May 31, 2030

(b) **Interest Payments**: All interest, dividends, distributions, and other payments now or hereafter payable with respect to the Pledged Securities.

(c) **Proceeds**: All proceeds of any of the foregoing, including cash proceeds, insurance proceeds, and any other proceeds.

(d) **Rights**: All rights, claims, and privileges of the Debtor with respect to the Pledged Securities.

(e) **Books and Records**: All books and records pertaining to the foregoing.

### 2.2 Continuing Security Interest

This Agreement shall create a continuing security interest in the Collateral and shall remain in full force and effect until all Obligations have been paid in full and the Facility has been terminated.

---

## ARTICLE 3: PERFECTION

### 3.1 Filing of Financing Statements

The Debtor authorizes the Secured Party to file one or more UCC financing statements (and continuation statements and amendments thereto) describing the Collateral in any jurisdiction where filing is necessary or advisable to perfect the security interest granted hereby.

### 3.2 Control Agreement

The Debtor shall execute and deliver, and shall cause the Transfer Agent to execute and deliver, a Control Agreement in form and substance satisfactory to the Secured Party, pursuant to which:

(a) The Transfer Agent acknowledges the Secured Party's security interest;

(b) The Transfer Agent agrees to comply with instructions from the Secured Party without further consent from the Debtor; and

(c) The Secured Party obtains "control" over the Pledged Securities for purposes of Article 8 and 9 of the UCC.

### 3.3 Delivery of Certificated Securities

If any of the Pledged Securities are represented by physical certificates, the Debtor shall deliver such certificates to the Secured Party, together with undated stock/bond powers executed in blank.

### 3.4 Further Assurances

The Debtor agrees to take any further action reasonably requested by the Secured Party to perfect, maintain, and protect the security interest granted hereby.
