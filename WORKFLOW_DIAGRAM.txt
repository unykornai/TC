│                STEP 4: COLLECT SIGNATURES                                │
│                     (2-5 business days)                                  │
└──────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────┘

   Each Party:
   1. Completes signature page
   2. Initials selected option (A or B)
   3. Signs document (digital or ink)
   4. Returns to counterparty
   
   Methods Available:
   • DocuSign / Adobe Sign (digital)
   • Print, sign, scan (ink)
   • Cryptographic signature (advanced)

   3. Pin signed package to IPFS
   4. Record CID
   5. Distribute CID to all parties
