## RECITALS

**A.** The Pledgor is the registered holder of certain TC Advantage 5% Secured Medium Term Notes, CUSIP 87225HAB4 (the "Securities"), for which the Transfer Agent serves as transfer agent and registrar.

**B.** The Pledgor has granted the Secured Party a security interest in the Securities pursuant to a Security Agreement of even date herewith.

**C.** The parties are entering into this Agreement to perfect the Secured Party's security interest in the Securities by "control" pursuant to Article 8 of the Uniform Commercial Code.

---

## ARTICLE 1: DEFINITIONS

### 1.1 Defined Terms

"**Account**" means the account maintained by the Transfer Agent in the name of the Pledgor reflecting the Pledgor's ownership of the Securities.

"**Default Notice**" means a written notice from the Secured Party to the Transfer Agent stating that an Event of Default has occurred under the Loan Documents.

"**Entitlement Orders**" means instructions directing the Transfer Agent to transfer, redeem, or otherwise dispose of the Securities.

"**Event of Default**" has the meaning given in the Facility Agreement between the Pledgor and the Secured Party.

"**Loan Documents**" means the Facility Agreement, Security Agreement, and related documents between the Pledgor and the Secured Party.

"**Securities**" means the TC Advantage 5% Secured Medium Term Notes, CUSIP 87225HAB4, ISIN US87225HAB42, with a face value of $10,000,000 and maturing May 31, 2030, and any other securities credited to the Account.

"**UCC**" means the Uniform Commercial Code as in effect in the State of Texas.

## ARTICLE 3: CONTROL

### 3.1 Agreement to Comply

The Transfer Agent hereby agrees that it will comply with Entitlement Orders originated by the Secured Party with respect to the Securities without further consent from the Pledgor.

### 3.2 Priority

The Transfer Agent acknowledges that:

(a) The Secured Party has been granted a security interest in the Securities;

(b) This Agreement establishes the Secured Party's "control" over the Securities for purposes of Article 8-106 and 9-106 of the UCC;

(c) The Secured Party's security interest has priority over any interest of the Transfer Agent in the Securities.

### 3.3 No Conflicting Agreements

The Transfer Agent represents that it has not entered into, and agrees that it will not enter into, any agreement with any other person relating to the Securities pursuant to which it has agreed, or will agree, to comply with entitlement orders of such other person.

---

## ARTICLE 4: OPERATION OF ACCOUNT

### 4.1 Prior to Default Notice

Until the Transfer Agent receives a Default Notice:

(a) The Pledgor may receive interest and other distributions on the Securities;

(b) The Pledgor may request informational statements regarding the Account;

(c) The Pledgor may NOT direct the Transfer Agent to transfer, sell, or otherwise dispose of the Securities without the prior written consent of the Secured Party.

### 4.2 After Default Notice

Upon receipt of a Default Notice:

(a) The Transfer Agent shall comply exclusively with Entitlement Orders from the Secured Party;

(b) The Transfer Agent shall not comply with any instruction from the Pledgor regarding the Securities without the Secured Party's written consent;

(c) The Transfer Agent shall remit all interest and other distributions to the Secured Party;

(d) The Transfer Agent shall execute any transfer or other disposition of the Securities as directed by the Secured Party.

### 4.3 Rescission of Default Notice

The Secured Party may rescind a Default Notice by delivering written notice to the Transfer Agent, whereupon the provisions of Section 4.1 shall again apply.

---

## ARTICLE 5: TRANSFER AGENT'S DUTIES

### 5.1 Standard of Care

The Transfer Agent shall maintain the Account and process transactions with reasonable care in accordance with its standard practices.

### 5.2 No Duty to Investigate

The Transfer Agent:

(a) Has no duty to investigate or confirm the occurrence of an Event of Default;

(b) May rely conclusively on any Default Notice or rescission delivered by the Secured Party;

(c) Has no duty to monitor the Loan Documents or the Pledgor's compliance therewith.

### 5.3 Statements

The Transfer Agent shall provide:

(a) To the Pledgor: periodic account statements in accordance with its standard practice;

(b) To the Secured Party: copies of all statements provided to the Pledgor, or upon request, confirmations of the Pledgor's position.

### 5.4 Voting

Until a Default Notice is received, the Pledgor shall be entitled to exercise any voting rights with respect to the Securities.

---

## ARTICLE 6: SECURED PARTY'S RIGHTS

### 6.1 Verification

The Secured Party may, at any time, request the Transfer Agent to confirm:

(a) The Pledgor's position in the Securities;

(b) Any restrictions or encumbrances on the Account;

(c) The effectiveness of this Agreement.

### 6.2 Notice of Adverse Claims

The Transfer Agent shall promptly notify the Secured Party of:

(a) Any claim, attachment, or levy against the Account;

(b) Any legal process served on the Transfer Agent affecting the Securities;

(c) Any notice of a lien or security interest from any other person.

### 6.3 Instructions

All Entitlement Orders and other instructions from the Secured Party shall be in writing (including email from authorized signers) and delivered in accordance with Section 8.

---

## ARTICLE 7: INDEMNIFICATION

### 7.1 By Pledgor

The Pledgor agrees to indemnify the Transfer Agent against any loss, claim, or expense (including reasonable attorneys' fees) arising from:

(a) The Transfer Agent's compliance with this Agreement;

(b) Any dispute between the Pledgor and the Secured Party;

(c) Any misrepresentation by the Pledgor hereunder.

### 7.2 By Secured Party

The Secured Party agrees to indemnify the Transfer Agent against any loss, claim, or expense (including reasonable attorneys' fees) arising from:

(a) The Transfer Agent's compliance with a Default Notice or Entitlement Order that the Secured Party was not entitled to deliver;

(b) Any wrongful instruction by the Secured Party.

### 7.3 Transfer Agent Liability

The Transfer Agent shall not be liable for any loss or damage arising from:

(a) Its good faith compliance with this Agreement;

(b) Any act or omission by the Pledgor or the Secured Party;

(c) Force majeure or circumstances beyond its reasonable control.

## ARTICLE 9: GENERAL PROVISIONS

### 9.1 Termination

This Agreement shall terminate upon the earliest of:

(a) Written agreement of all parties;

(b) Receipt by the Transfer Agent of written notice from the Secured Party that the security interest has been released; or

(c) Transfer of all Securities out of the Account in accordance with instructions from the Secured Party.

### 9.2 Governing Law

This Agreement shall be governed by and construed in accordance with the laws of the State of Texas.

### 9.3 Jurisdiction

The parties submit to the jurisdiction of the courts of [JURISDICTION] for the resolution of any disputes arising hereunder.

### 9.4 Amendments

No amendment, modification, or waiver of any provision of this Agreement shall be effective unless in writing and signed by all parties.

### 9.5 Assignment

The Secured Party may assign its rights hereunder to any assignee of its interest under the Loan Documents, upon notice to the Transfer Agent and the Pledgor.

### 9.6 Successors

This Agreement shall be binding upon and inure to the benefit of the parties and their respective successors and assigns.

### 9.7 Severability

If any provision of this Agreement is held invalid or unenforceable, the remaining provisions shall remain in full force and effect.

### 9.8 Entire Agreement

This Agreement constitutes the entire agreement among the parties with respect to control of the Securities.

### 9.9 Counterparts

This Agreement may be executed in counterparts, each of which shall be deemed an original.

### 9.10 Transfer Agent Fees

The Pledgor shall pay all fees and expenses of the Transfer Agent in connection with this Agreement and the services provided hereunder.
