## Purpose

This Note is issued **in consideration for platform architecture, system design, engineering, deployment, testing, documentation, and delivery services already rendered** by the Sponsor.

This instrument represents **deferred consideration for value delivered**, not a loan of cash. The services have been **substantially performed** as evidenced by the deployed OPTKAS sovereign financial platform.

## Key Structural Features

### Debt Classification
- Valid, binding, enforceable **debt obligation**
- Not equity, not profit participation, not investment
- No voting, governance, or management rights

### Independence from Investor Capital
- Payable from operating revenues or general assets
- Does not prime investor collateral
- Does not touch escrowed investor funds
- Does not impair bond issuance program

### Financeable Paper
- Fully assignable, pledgeable, financeable
- No issuer consent required for assignment
- Issuer provides reasonable confirmations to financing counterparties
- PIK interest option provides cash flow flexibility

### Acceleration Protection
- Payment failure → acceleration
- Material breach → acceleration
- Platform rights termination → acceleration
- Insolvency / dissolution → acceleration

### No Setoff
- Cannot be netted against transaction participation fees
- Cannot be reduced by counterclaim
