# SPONSOR AGREEMENT CLAUSES

**Purpose:** Core agreement sections governing the relationship between OPTKAS1-MAIN SPV (Issuer) and Unykorn 7777, Inc. (Platform Sponsor). These clauses are designed for insertion into the Strategic Infrastructure Execution Agreement or a standalone Sponsor Agreement.

**Legal Standard:** Institutional-grade, lender-defensible, auditor-compliant.

---

## SECTION 1 — ROLE & CAPACITY OF UNYKORN (FOUNDATIONAL CLAUSE)

### 1.1 Independent Platform Provider

The Parties acknowledge and agree that **Unykorn 7777, Inc. ("Unykorn")** is acting solely in its capacity as an **independent platform architect, system builder, and infrastructure provider**, and **not** as an issuer, underwriter, placement agent, broker, dealer, fiduciary, investment adviser, or manager of investor funds.

### 1.2 No Fiduciary or Agency Relationship

Nothing in this Agreement shall be deemed to create any partnership, joint venture, fiduciary duty, agency relationship, or investment advisory relationship between Unykorn and the Issuer, the SPV, or any investors.

### 1.3 No Broker-Dealer Activity

Unykorn shall not be deemed a fiduciary, investment adviser, broker, dealer, placement agent, or underwriter with respect to any financing transaction, and shall have no responsibility for investor suitability, disclosures, or investment outcomes.

---

## SECTION 2 — SYSTEM BUILD & DEPLOYMENT FEES

### 2.1 Platform Design and Deployment Fees

In consideration for the design, engineering, implementation, testing, documentation, and deployment of the OPTKAS sovereign financial platform (the "Platform"), the Issuer shall pay Unykorn the fees set forth in **Schedule A** (the "Platform Fees").

### 2.2 Non-Contingent Fees

The Platform Fees are **earned upon performance**, are **not contingent upon the success of any financing or bond issuance**, and shall be payable in accordance with the milestone schedule agreed by the Parties.

---

## SECTION 3 — SPONSOR CONSIDERATION INSTRUMENT

### 3.1 Issuance

In partial consideration for the Platform design, architecture, engineering, and delivery services rendered by Unykorn, the Issuer shall issue to Unykorn a **Sponsor Consideration Note** (the "Sponsor Note") on the terms set forth in **Schedule B**.

### 3.2 Nature of Instrument

The Sponsor Note shall constitute a **bona fide debt obligation** of the Issuer, representing deferred consideration for services already rendered, and shall **not** constitute equity, a profit participation interest, or an investment in the Issuer.

### 3.3 Independence from Investor Capital

The Sponsor Note is **independent of any bond issuance, investor proceeds, escrowed funds, or trust assets**, and shall not impair or subordinate any obligations owed to bondholders or investors, except as expressly disclosed.

### 3.4 Assignment and Financing Rights

Unykorn may **assign, pledge, finance, discount, or otherwise encumber** the Sponsor Note, in whole or in part, **without the consent of the Issuer**, for purposes of obtaining third-party financing.

### 3.5 Survival

The Sponsor Note shall survive termination of this Agreement for any reason.

---

## SECTION 4 — PLATFORM LICENSE

### 4.1 License Grant

Subject to the terms of this Agreement, Unykorn grants the Issuer a **limited, non-exclusive, non-transferable, non-sublicensable license** to operate the Platform **solely for the Issuer's internal financing activities** within the scope expressly permitted herein.

### 4.2 Reservation of Rights

All right, title, and interest in and to the Platform, including its architecture, methodologies, workflows, codebase, derivatives, enhancements, and future modules, are and shall remain the exclusive property of Unykorn.

### 4.3 Future Enhancements

Any additional systems, modules, capabilities, or expansions beyond the Platform as delivered shall require a **separate written statement of work and commercial terms**.

---

## SECTION 5 — PLATFORM UTILIZATION FEE (10% PARTICIPATION)

### 5.1 Transaction Participation Fee

For each financing transaction executed through the Platform, the Issuer shall pay Unykorn a **platform utilization and execution fee** equal to **ten percent (10%)** of the applicable transaction amount, as set forth in **Schedule C**.

### 5.2 No Capital Raising Activity

The Parties expressly acknowledge that such fee compensates Unykorn **solely for platform access, transaction execution, settlement coordination, and system utilization**, and **not** for investor solicitation, placement services, or capital raising activities.

### 5.3 Fee Language Clarification

Fees are payable for platform access, execution, settlement, and infrastructure services, and not for capital placement or investor solicitation.

---

## SECTION 6 — ACCELERATION PROTECTION

### 6.1 Acceleration Upon Improper Termination

In the event the Issuer materially breaches this Agreement or terminates Unykorn's platform rights other than for cause, all outstanding amounts owed under the Sponsor Note shall immediately accelerate and become due and payable.

### 6.2 Acceleration Events

The Sponsor Note shall also accelerate upon:

- Insolvency, dissolution, or assignment for the benefit of creditors by the Issuer
- Unauthorized replacement of the Platform or its core modules
- Failure to pay any amount when due under this Agreement or the Sponsor Note

---

## SECTION 7 — NON-SETOFF

### 7.1 Independent Obligations

The Issuer's obligations under the Sponsor Note are **independent of and shall not be subject to any setoff, counterclaim, or deduction** arising from any other provision of this Agreement, including without limitation the 10% transaction participation fee.

## SECTION 9 — SPONSOR POSTURE SUMMARY

Unykorn 7777, Inc. may be:
- ✅ A **builder** and system architect
- ✅ A **licensor** of platform technology
- ✅ A **platform operator** providing execution services
- ✅ A **creditor** holding a Sponsor Note

Unykorn 7777, Inc. must **not** be:
- ❌ A capital raiser or investor solicitor
- ❌ A fiduciary to the SPV or its investors
- ❌ A shadow general partner or manager of investor funds
- ❌ An unregistered broker, dealer, or placement agent
